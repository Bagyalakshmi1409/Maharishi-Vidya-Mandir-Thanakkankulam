Maharishi Vidya Mandir Senior Secondary School

Located in Thanakkankulam, Madurai, is a co-educational institution established in 2012. Affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), the school offers classes from Nursery to XII, with English as the medium of instruction.

About Us

In the year 2012, on June 14th, the school was ready to nestle children from Pre K.G. to Std XII. 116 walked in and were welcomed by a crew of 19 teachers. Though the cozy classrooms and the incredible infrastructure attracted everyone, it was the motherly care given by the dedicated teachers that attracted children from various directions. In the second year, 18 teachers stepped in newly, and 414 students were added to the MVM family. With the untiring efforts of the Management, Principal, and staff, the big banyan tree has spread its branches further to shelter 1000+ students.

Academic and Co-Curricular Activities

The school emphasizes holistic development through various co-curricular activities, including:

  • Hobby classes, debates, drama, educational tours, and creative writing.
  • Competitions such as recitation, music, quiz, Olympiads, and art contests.
  • Sports and fitness programs, including indoor games, yoga, and aerobics.

Additionally, the curriculum integrates Transcendental Meditation, Yogasana, and Pranayama to promote mental well-being.

Facilities:

  • Smart classrooms, well-equipped science and computer labs, and a library.
  • Sports facilities, a cafeteria, and transport services with GPS tracking and CCTV surveillance.
